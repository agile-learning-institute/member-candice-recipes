Sloe Gin Fizz Cocktail

Here's a great cocktail for your Mardi Gras party!

This refreshing cocktail will surely be a hit at your Mardi Gras party!

Description

Sloe gin, a blackthorn plum-flavored spirit, gets a dose of fizzy club soda, sugar, and lemon juice in this tart and refreshing cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 ice cubes
  • 2 fluid ounces sloe gin
  • 1 fluid ounce lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon simple syrup
  • 1 cup ice
  • 4 fluid ounces club soda
  • 1 slice lemon

Steps

  1. Place 2 ice cubes into a highball glass and set aside in the freezer.
  2. Combine sloe gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add 1 cup ice; cover and shake until the outside of the shaker is frosted. Strain into the prepared highball glass.
  3. Stir in club soda and garnish with a slice of lemon.
